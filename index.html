1 · Resume Shock

Live demo

Turn a PDF résumé into a clean, shareable web résumé — parsed entirely in your browser, nothing uploaded.

Live Demo →
Case study

Problem. A PDF résumé is hard to share nicely, and résumé-site tools want an account and an upload.

Desktop app

Landing & Download →
Case study
